INDIAN CENTRE OF SOCIAL SCIENCE AND MANAGEMENT RESEARCH

Author Guidelines

Submit your paper at submission page.

There is no article processing charge for publishing in the "Journal of Mechanical Engineering Advancements".

Journal of Mechanical Engineering Advancements call for original research papers, survey papers, review articles, case studies and academic or scholarly articles.

Prior to submitting your paper, please follow the instructions given below. Please note that you must have an e-mail address to use the online submission system. Authors submit their article online simply by registering, logging-in and submitting. Editors will then invite potential reviewers by e-mail.

Submission Checklist

You can use this list to carry out a final check of your submission before you send it to the journal for review. Please check the relevant section in this Guide for Authors for more details.

Ensure that the following items are present:

  • One author has been designated as the corresponding author with contact details:
  • E-mail address
  • Full postal address
  • Cover letter (in pdf format)
  • Manuscript (in .doc file, word format only)

All necessary files have been uploaded included in Manuscript:

  • Include keywords
  • All figures (include relevant captions)
  • All tables (including titles, description, footnotes)
  • Ensure all figure and table citations in the text match the files provided

Further considerations

  • Manuscript has been 'spell checked' and 'grammar checked'
  • All references mentioned in the Reference List are cited in the text, and vice versa
  • Permission has been obtained for use of copyrighted material from other sources (including the Internet)
  • A competing interests statement is provided, even if the authors have no competing interests to declare
  • Journal policies detailed in this guide have been reviewed
  • Referee suggestions and contact details provided, based on journal requirements

Originality and Plagiarism

Authors should ensure that they have written entirely original works, and if the authors have used the work and/or words of others this must be appropriately cited or quoted. Referred contents from different sources must not be plagiarised and should follow the guidelines of journal of Mechanical Engineering Advancements. Publisher is committed to maintain robust plagiarism detection to ensure research integrity.

Overview of Production Process

  • Submission of Manuscript
  • Plagiarism Check
  • Passed/Reject Manuscript from Editorial
  • Review Process
  • Acceptance/Modification/Rejection (Subjected to reviewers decision)
  • Proof check
  • Publish
