Aims & Scope

Swami Vivekananda University, an integral component of a prominent educational consortium, was established with the objective of fostering national development through research, innovation, and high-quality education. Spearheaded by Dr. Nandan Gupta, Chancellor, whose expertise and foresight have elevated Swami Vivekananda University into a premier institution of learning.

The "Journal of Mechanical Engineering Advancements," explores the uncharted territories of mechanical engineering excellence and publishes high-quality articles/review papers/case studies in mechanical engineering, multi-disciplinary engineering sciences. We pride ourselves on being a platform for groundbreaking research and innovation at the engineering forefront, seeking to publish the most promising findings across various domains.

"Journal of Mechanical Engineering Advancements, is a peer-reviewed open-access e-journal encompasses various branches of Mechanical Engineering.
