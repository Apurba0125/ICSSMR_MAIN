Welcome to ICSSMR

The Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research (ICSSMR) envisions becoming a premier national and international hub for excellence in education, research, innovation, and knowledge dissemination in the fields of Management and Social Sciences.

Our vision is to empower individuals and institutions through quality research, value-based education, and intellectual collaboration that bridges the gap between theory and practice. We aim to nurture a research-driven ecosystem where academicians, researchers, students, and industry experts can work collectively to address complex social, economic, and managerial challenges. ICSSMR aspires to contribute to the creation of an inclusive and sustainable knowledge society, where research is not only confined to academic boundaries but also generates meaningful impact for communities, industries, and policy-making bodies. By fostering innovation, ethical leadership, and interdisciplinary learning, ICSSMR seeks to redefine the standards of higher education and research excellence in India and beyond.

Our Certification Programs

The Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research (ICSSMR) offers comprehensive Post Graduate Certificate programs designed to enhance your career. Our specialized certifications in Business Administration, Healthcare Management, Human Resource Management, Banking and Investment Management, and more are crafted to provide industry-relevant skills and knowledge.

Each program features validity periods ranging from 1 to 3 years and caters to all levels—from beginners to advanced professionals. We aim to empower individuals through quality education and research-driven learning that bridges the gap between theory and practice.
