"The Management of Today is The Leadership of Tomorrow"

About ICSSMR

The Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research (ICSSMR) is a national-level autonomous organization registered under the Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Government of India. It is dedicated to advancing excellence in social science and management education, research, and innovation. ICSSMR provides a dynamic platform for academicians, researchers, professionals, and students to collaborate, exchange knowledge, and contribute to national and global development through meaningful academic and professional activities.

At ICSSMR, we believe that quality research and education are the foundation of societal progress. Our initiatives are designed to bridge the gap between academia and practice, enabling students, scholars, and institutions to enhance their skills, capabilities, and impact.

Core Services

Certificate and Training Programs

ICSSMR conducts skill-oriented certificate, diploma, and professional development programs across diverse areas of management and social sciences. These programs are tailored to meet the evolving academic and industry demands, helping participants enhance their analytical, research, and managerial skills. Specialized workshops and certification courses are also offered for faculty members, research scholars, and professionals seeking to stay updated with modern tools, methodologies, and trends.

Internship Program

The ICSSMR Internship Program is designed for undergraduate and postgraduate students as well as research scholars, offering them real-time exposure to research, academic documentation, project writing, and data analysis. The program blends theoretical learning with practical experience, preparing participants for academic, corporate, and research careers.

Publication of Books and Research Journals

ICSSMR publishes a series of peer-reviewed journals, edited volumes, and academic books in the fields of management, economics, commerce, and social sciences. These publications provide a credible platform for scholars to showcase their original work, contributing to academic excellence and the dissemination of innovative ideas.

Research & Innovation

We promote a culture of research excellence and innovation by supporting interdisciplinary studies, research collaborations, and evidence-based policy analysis. ICSSMR encourages faculty members, students, and independent researchers to undertake innovative projects that address emerging social, economic, and managerial issues at regional, national, and global levels.

Funded Research Grants

ICSSMR provides research grants and funding support to scholars and institutions engaged in pioneering studies in management and social sciences. These grants are awarded based on the originality, social relevance, and expected impact of the proposed research, thereby encouraging innovation and academic contribution.

Quality Assessments and Ranking Framework for HEIs

To ensure academic quality and institutional growth, ICSSMR conducts quality assessments, accreditation support, and ranking frameworks for higher education institutions (HEIs). Our evaluation model is based on parameters such as research output, teaching-learning processes, governance, infrastructure, innovation, and community engagement. The framework assists institutions in benchmarking and improving their overall performance.

Membership Programs

ICSSMR offers Individual and Institutional Memberships to academicians, students, researchers, and corporate professionals. Members receive exclusive access to ICSSMR events, training, publications, and funding opportunities. Membership provides a platform to network, collaborate, and stay connected with a national and global community of scholars and practitioners.

Institutional Collaborations

Through Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) and strategic partnerships with universities, colleges, and research organizations, ICSSMR establishes Centres of Excellence in Research and Innovation. These collaborations promote joint research projects, faculty exchange, academic audits, and collaborative conferences.

Academic Events

ICSSMR regularly organizes webinars, seminars, conferences, symposiums, workshops, and panel discussions to promote intellectual engagement and knowledge sharing. These events provide opportunities for participants to interact with leading experts, present research papers, and exchange ideas on emerging topics in management and social sciences.

Awards and Recognition

ICSSMR recognizes and celebrates academic and professional excellence through its annual awards, fellowships, and recognitions. These honours are conferred upon outstanding individuals and institutions that have made significant contributions to research, education, innovation, and leadership in the field of management and social sciences.
