About ICSSMR Membership

Membership with the Indian Centre for Social Science and Management Research (ICSSMR) is a mark of professional recognition, awarded to individuals who demonstrate commitment and excellence in the fields of management and social sciences. As a member, you gain access to a collaborative platform that brings together academicians, researchers, professionals, and students from across India and beyond.
